role: front-end developer
During project week at Google's Computer Science Summer Institute, my group and I created Tack It! This is a virtual bulletin board that is unique to each user. After siging in with a Google account, you may create post-it notes and upload pictures (that are then displayed as 'buttons'). An easy way to keep track of notes, to-do lists, ideas, and any other text!


role: software engineer
At HackIllinois, my group & I hacked the R/C controller of a Nerf machine and connected it to an Arduino to make fully automatic. Using different computer vision algorithms, the high definition web camera attached to the turret can track and target faces. Its constant rate of fire rains down foamy doom upon those who dare challenge its powers.
